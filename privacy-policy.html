Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 16, 2026

Tourbillion Energy ("Company," "we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you visit our website or submit a request through our lead form.

1. Information We Collect

When you submit our consultation request form, we may collect:

  • Full name
  • Phone number
  • ZIP code
  • Any additional information you voluntarily provide

We also automatically collect standard technical data such as your IP address, browser type, and pages visited via cookies and analytics tools (including the Meta Pixel).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to:

  • Contact you by phone, text, or email about solar energy options for your home
  • Schedule and conduct energy consultations
  • Respond to your inquiries
  • Improve our services and marketing

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

3. Meta Pixel & Advertising

Our website uses the Meta Pixel (Facebook/Instagram tracking technology) to measure the effectiveness of our advertising and to reach people who may be interested in our services. This may involve sharing certain browsing activity data with Meta Platforms, Inc. You can opt out of Meta's advertising tracking at facebook.com/privacy.

4. Text Messaging

By submitting our form, you consent to receive calls and/or text messages from Tourbillion Energy regarding your consultation request. Message and data rates may apply. You may opt out at any time by replying STOP to any text message or contacting us directly.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy or as required by law.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your information
  • Opt out of marketing communications at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the information below.

7. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party sites (such as Think Energy). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites and encourage you to review their policies separately.

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
